## Project Overview
R3 Restomod had compelling visual content, but visitors were not consistently moving from galleries to consultation actions.

## What We Changed
- Rebuilt gallery and project pages around conversion intent.
- Added clearer consultation CTAs at key decision points.
- Improved page performance for media-heavy layouts.

## Outcome
The final experience preserved brand style while generating more consultation-ready leads from portfolio traffic.

## Project Overview

One Hundred King is a major redevelopment project in downtown Chatham led by Rob Myers and his team. As the project prepared for its public reveal, a website was needed to introduce the development and generate interest from potential retail tenants. I collaborated with the Exordia Creative team to design and launch a conversion-focused coming soon page that could clearly present the project while capturing inquiries from businesses interested in leasing space.

The project moved quickly due to the upcoming public announcement.

## What We Built

The website was designed and launched in under ten days, focusing on clarity, credibility, and conversion for commercial tenants.

- Designed a focused launch landing page for the redevelopment project.
- Structured messaging to appeal to retail and commercial tenants.
- Built clear inquiry pathways for leasing interest.
- Supported the public announcement of the development project.

## Outcome

Following the public reveal, the website received significant traffic as the project was introduced to the community.

- Over 20,000 visitors accessed the site during the initial launch period.
- The landing page helped introduce the redevelopment to potential retail partners.
- The project established an early digital presence for the One Hundred King development.

The building is currently nearing completion, with the redevelopment expected to be finished in 2026.

## Project Overview

Enhanced Concrete was created as an expansion of an existing landscaping business. The goal was to establish a separate identity focused specifically on concrete services while maintaining the reputation the contractor had already built locally. This project involved creating the brand and website from the ground up to support the new direction of the business.

## What We Built

The site was designed to clearly communicate the contractor’s services while encouraging visitors to request quotes or schedule work.

- Developed the brand identity and logo for the new concrete division.
- Designed and built a conversion-focused contractor website.
- Created individual service pages for each concrete offering.
- Structured the site to support local SEO and service-based searches.
- Integrated a booking system so customers could easily request work or consultations.

## Outcome

The finished website provided a strong digital foundation for the new concrete division while supporting ongoing local SEO growth.

- Clear separation between landscaping and concrete services.
- Stronger service presentation for high-intent search visitors.
- A streamlined booking experience designed to convert website traffic into leads.

## Project Overview
Davey Chiro had solid traffic but inconsistent conversion behavior, especially on mobile. Visitors often had questions but were not taking the final booking step.
This project was delivered in collaboration with Exordia Creative.

## What We Changed
- Simplified service page layout and CTA flow.
- Added trust elements and clearer care-plan framing.
- Reduced friction in the booking path and contact options.

## Outcome
The updated site produced stronger appointment conversion rates and a clearer visitor journey from discovery to inquiry.
